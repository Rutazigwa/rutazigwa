The Best Movies According to Rutazigwa

My Top 3 Movies of This Year

Oppenheimer

A gripping historical drama that delves into the life of J. Robert Oppenheimer and the creation of the atomic bomb. A masterpiece of storytelling and cinematography.

Dune: Part Two

An epic continuation of the Dune saga, this movie brings the rich, intricate world of Arrakis to life with stunning visuals and a powerful narrative.

Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse

A visually spectacular animated film that explores multiple dimensions with Spider-Man variants, filled with action, heart, and groundbreaking animation.
